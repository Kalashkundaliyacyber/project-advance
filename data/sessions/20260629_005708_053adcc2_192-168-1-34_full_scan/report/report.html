4. Assessment Overview

On June 29, 2026, TWS used the ThreatWeave Security platform to evaluate the security posture of Lab Assessment's infrastructure against current industry best practices, including a Full Scan of the in-scope target(s). Methodology is based on the NIST SP 800-115 Technical Guide to Information Security Testing and Assessment, the OWASP Testing Guide, and ThreatWeave Security's own automated testing framework. Phases of the assessment include the following:

  • Planning – Assessment goals, scope, and rules of engagement are established.
  • Discovery – Scanning and enumeration are performed to identify potential vulnerabilities and weak areas.
  • Correlation – Discovered service versions are matched against known CVE databases and scored for risk.
  • Reporting – All findings, version intelligence, and remediation guidance are documented in this report.

Full Scan – this assessment used an automated security scan of the in-scope target(s). The assessment lasted approximately 25 min 37 sec and covered 1 live host(s).
